The purpose of that challenge is to finish all 30 tasks in 30 days Access to that challenge is free.

On my page you will find a panel from where you can go to the different tasks, each contains short description what you have to do in that particular task. Under that description you will find my code.

Challenge 4 : Array Cardio Day 1

The following tasks should be performed on the following objects::


  1. Filter the list of inventions for those born in 1500
  2. Provide an array of names and surnames of inventors
  3. Sort inventors by date of birth, from oldest to youngest
  4. How many years did all inventors live together?
  5. Sort inventors by years
  6. make a list of Paris boulevards that contain "de" anywhere in their name
    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Boulevards_in_Paris
  7. Sort people alphabetically by last name
  8. Sum the occurrences of each of them (data array)
